About Me

Born and raised in Ventura, with a passion for caputuring moments to insprie others. As a child, I loved running around with my moms camera snapping photos of every flower and bug I could find in our backyard. As I got older and I grew to love capturing landscapes as the sunrised or my family playing at the park. For me, each photo represented a story of a moment in my life. Looking back at those photos I feel so much joy and happiness. I knew I wanted to share this feeling with the world. That's when I decided to open KC Phototgraphy. My portfolio showcases some favorite images from the past few years; I strive to create photographs that not only capture a moment, but also capture a feeling and essence.

I am available for private portrait sessions, as well as events in the Ventura and Santa Barbara areas -- though I would also be very interested in working on location elsewhere.

I'd love to hear from you, so feel free to contact me.

Thank you for taking time to look at my site and work; I hope to meet you in the near future.
